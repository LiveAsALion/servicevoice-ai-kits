## EMAIL 3 — EDUCATION (Day 5)

**Subject:** The one thing that kills most AI agent deployments

---

Malcolm here.

I've seen a lot of service businesses try AI phone agents and give up in the first week. Here's why:

**They set it up and never listen to the recordings.**

Retell AI logs every call. In your dashboard, you can hear exactly what your agent said and what the caller heard. Most people never check it.

In the first week, you should listen to every call. Not because something is broken — but because you'll hear the 2–3 adjustments that make the agent sound exactly right for your business.

The most common things to fix:
- The greeting sounds too generic → add your business name and a specific line about what you do
- The agent is collecting info in the wrong order for your workflow → reorder the questions
- Callers are hanging up before giving their address → add a line like "I just need one quick thing before I book you"

Small prompt edits. Big difference in how natural it sounds.

Spend 30 minutes this week in your Retell dashboard. You'll know exactly what to change.

— Malcolm

P.S. — If you haven't deployed yet, no judgment. Reply and tell me where you're stuck. That's what I'm here for.
