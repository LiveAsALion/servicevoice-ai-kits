## Brand Voice

**Who we're talking to:** The shift worker who eats garbage from a vending machine at 3am not because they don't care — but because they had zero time to prepare and their body is running on fumes.

**Tone:** Direct. Competent. Warm but no-nonsense. Like advice from a partner who's been there. NOT preachy. NOT "clean eating influencer." NOT fitness bro.

**Faith angle:** Integrated, not performative. "God gave you one body. Treat it like it matters." — occasional, authentic, never preachy.

**Sample phrases:**
- "12-hour shift. 115 degrees. Here's what I ate."
- "No cooler? No excuses. 5 options that survive your patrol bag."
- "Your family deserves a version of you that isn't running on gas station food."
- "This isn't meal prep. This is logistics."

## Content Pillars (Rotate)

1. **THE PREP** — Sunday meal prep process, what's in the containers, actual quantities, macros
2. **THE SHIFT** — What it actually looks like eating on patrol/K-9 duty; real conditions
3. **THE SCIENCE** — Why shift work destroys your metabolism, how to counter it, evidence-based
4. **THE KIT** — Gear: containers, cooler bags, sporks, whatever keeps food alive on a 12-hour
5. **THE STORY** — Faith, family, why you do this, the harder days, what keeps you disciplined
6. **THE QUESTION** — Community engagement; ask followers what they struggle with, polls, replies

## Monetization Sequence

### Phase 1 (0–1,000 followers): Build trust, zero sales
- Consistent posting (4–5x/week across platforms)
- Affiliate links in bio for gear/containers only (low-friction, high-relevance)
- Build email list from day one — free "Shift Prep Starter Guide" PDF as lead magnet

### Phase 2 (1,000–5,000 followers): Introduce affiliate programs
- Apply to supplement affiliate programs: Raw Nutrition, ReviveMD, Thorne, Legion
- Amazon Associates for containers, cooler bags, meal prep tools
- Promote authentically — only what you actually use

### Phase 3 (5,000–15,000 followers): First paid digital product
- "The Duty Prep System" — $27 PDF guide: 4-week rotating meal system for shift workers
- Keep it practical, not aspirational. Recipes that survive heat, work in a patrol bag, require minimal prep.
- Sell through Lemon Squeezy (already in your tool stack)

### Phase 4 (15,000+ followers): Sponsorships
- Outreach to brands already sponsoring LEO fitness accounts
- Target: supplement brands, container/cooler brands, tactical kitchen gear
- First year target: 1–2 brand partnerships @ $500–$2,000/post
