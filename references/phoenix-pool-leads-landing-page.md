## Layout & Structure (Top to Bottom)

1. **Hero / Tagline**
   - Short punchy headline + subtext
   - Single CTA button: "Get a Fast Quote" (anchors to contact form)

2. **Credibility Section — "Why Phoenix Homeowners Trust Us"**
   - 3-column value props (icons implied, not confirmed):
     - Local & Human — neighbor framing, not corporate
     - Vetted Pros — reliability and fair pricing angle
     - Speed — fast match/alert system

3. **How It Works — 3-Step Process**
   - Step 1: Tell us what you need (describe the pool issue via form)
   - Step 2: We match you (personal neighborhood match — Scottsdale, Ahwatukee, Cave Creek, etc.)
   - Step 3: Job Done (quote + resolution within 24 hours)

4. **Service Areas**
   - Flat text list: Phoenix • Scottsdale • Tempe • Mesa • Chandler • Gilbert • Cave Creek • Peoria • Glendale

5. **Contact / Quote Form**
   - Headline: "Get a Fast Quote"
   - Standard form: name, need description, location

- First-person persona ("I'm Alex") — local, human, trustworthy
- Anti-corporate framing — positions as a neighbor, not a directory
- Fast and problem-aware (pump dies in July framing resonates with Phoenix audience)
